Frequently Asked Questions

Is running the extension in Developer Mode safe?
Yes, absolutely. Running local unpacked extensions is the standard process for web developers and early testers. Because you compile/extract the files directly on your machine, you have complete control and transparency over the codebase.
How do I get new updates in Developer Mode?
When we release updates, you will receive a notification. Simply download the updated `.zip` file, extract it to the exact same folder to overwrite the old files, and click the Refresh (circular arrow) icon inside your browser's chrome://extensions dashboard. No reinstall required!
Does Oryonix AI store my private browsing history?
No. Privacy is our founding principle. Oryonix AI operates directly in your local browser sandbox. All vector embeddings, AI reasoning steps, and page analyses are stored locally in secure IndexedDB storage. No logs or private histories ever leave your device.
