2. Core Operational Principles & Future BYOK Support

The Software operates strictly as a client-side tool. By default, all browser perception, DOM analysis, and agentic workflows are executed locally on your machine. Once the BYOK feature is active, when configuring cloud LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Groq, Mistral), your API credentials will be stored securely in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local), and you will be solely responsible for all bandwidth, token usage, and billing costs generated through your configured keys.
